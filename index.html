Unique Salonga

Unique Salonga is a Filipino singer-songwriter known for his distinctive voice and meaningful lyrics. Starting his career as the lead vocalist of IV of Spades, Unique later pursued a successful solo path, captivating audiences with his unique blend of indie, pop, and alternative rock.

Singer Song-Writer

Unique Salonga has made waves in the Philippine music scene with his heartfelt and original compositions. His talent for creating deeply emotional songs has earned him both critical and commercial success. Breaking through with the hit song "Mundo," Unique continues to surprise fans with his artistic growth and memorable performances.

Unique at a young age

Born Unique Torralba Salonga on April 26, 2000, to Randy Salonga and Llewelyn Torralba, Unique grew up with a passion for music. He began experimenting with songwriting and performing at a young age, eventually finding his way to the spotlight as a member of IV of Spades. His transition to a solo career has allowed him to fully express his creative vision.
